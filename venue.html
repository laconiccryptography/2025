Venue

The affiliated events will take place on May 4, 2025, at Facultad de Ciencias Matemáticas, Universidad Complutense de Madrid (UCM) which is located at Plaza de Ciencias 3, Ciudad Universitaria. 28040 - Madrid (España). Please check the official website for the most up to date information.

Workshop registration will be via the Eurocrypt 2025 registration process with the options to register for the individual workshop days only or for workshops along with the main conference.
